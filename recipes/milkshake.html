Milkshake

Think about adding some blended mystery meat into this recipe to add a little spice in your life! Or sadness. Either or, really.

Ingredients

  • Milk, any animal
  • Icecream, any flavor

Optional Ingredients

  • A hotdog - raw, cooked, or still squirming.

Method

  1. Combine all ingredients.
  2. Blend until it's so smooth that it makes you uncomfortable to look at.

Special Equipment

  • Blender
