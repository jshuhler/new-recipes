Hotdog

Tubes of meat of mysterious origins!

Ingredients

  • Hotdogs, meat source optional
  • Buns
  • Toppings, whatever you want I don't care

Method

  1. Heat the hotdogs if you want. You can eat them cold, I think?
  2. Slip that baby in between those buns
    • Note: You can toast those buns up if you want to.
  3. Top that bad boy up with the stuff you've gathered.
  4. See how much you can eat in one bite.
